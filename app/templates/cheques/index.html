How it works
  1. Each cheque scan is read for its cheque number, customer name (the drawer/account holder), amount and date.
  2. Each cheque number is searched across all the bank statements you upload.
  3. Where it appears, you see the bank, the amount per statement and the transaction date — so you can confirm the cheque cleared.
