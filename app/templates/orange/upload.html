How it works

  1. Upload the monthly statement — failed (“Echec”) transactions and outgoing C2C transfers are dropped automatically; only successful collections (credits) are kept.
  2. Review the collections grouped by correspondant and confirm or add each customer’s name and account number — known ones are pre-filled ({{ saved_count }} saved so far).
  3. Generate — every successful payment becomes an Orange-branded receipt, bundled into a single ZIP with one folder per correspondant.

Names & accounts are remembered by correspondant number, so each upload identifies more customers automatically. View saved customers →
