What is checked
  1. Forme — Art. 150 (5) CGI: supplier & client NIU, invoice date, supplier name / address / RCCM, client identity, transaction detail, HT, VAT rate & amount (or « Exonérée »), TTC, and the DGI e-invoicing marker (Art. 8 bis (2) tiret 2).
  2. Fond: supplier on the active-taxpayer file (verified live on DGI Fiscalis — fiscalis.dgi.cm, same logic as the Vendor NIU tool), no cash payment at ≥ FCFA 100,000 (Art. 8 bis (1)), tax-haven exposure (Art. 8 ter), plus réalité / rattachement / non-exagération and the specific caps raised as reviewer actions.
  3. Billed to DHL: the client NIU and client legal name printed on the invoice must match DHL's taxpayer ID and raison sociale configured by the admin under Settings → Company identity — a mismatch fails the invoice.
  4. Fournisseurs étrangers: the Art. 150 (5) formalism is waived (Art. 8 bis (2) tiret 1) — flagged for the substance checks instead.
