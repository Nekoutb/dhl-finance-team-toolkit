What the analysis does
  1. Reads each invoice and computes days past due (using the due date, or invoice date + 15 days / 7 days for duty & VAT).
  2. Maps each customer to its treatment plan (Telesales/Multichannel, Field Sales & Major, Enterprise, New) and finds the action now due on that plan's timeline.
  3. Rolls up per customer: total AR, % past due beyond GCTP, credit-limit overrun → credit-risk control (watch / hold / stop).
  4. Suppresses collection calls below the country value threshold.
