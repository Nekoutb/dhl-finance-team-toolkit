Collections Treatment Plan (CtP) monitoring. Feed the accounts-receivable breakdown at invoice level; the app analyses each open item against the Collections Treatment Plan and generates the series of controls to apply.

Draft. The exact Collections Treatment Plan you provide will configure the thresholds, actions and owners below. The logic shown here is derived from the Credit Policy — Express Specific Conditions v3.3 as a starting point, so you can react to a concrete proposal.

How it will work

  1. Feed data. Upload the AR breakdown at invoice level. Expected fields: customer / account, invoice no., invoice date, due date, invoice type (freight vs duty/VAT), amount & currency, sales segment, country rank, credit limit.
  2. Analyse per CtP. For each item the app computes days past due (vs due date), aging bucket, share of total AR past due, and credit-limit utilisation, then maps the customer to its treatment plan (Telesales/Multichannel, Field Sales & Major, Enterprise, or New Customer).
  3. Generate controls. It outputs a series of controls — per invoice and per customer — listing the trigger, the action due, and the responsible owner, with anything below the call value-threshold suppressed.
