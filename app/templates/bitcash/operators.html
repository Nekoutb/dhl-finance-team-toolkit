👥 Operator statements — IRO returns

Each reseller account (the SAP account in the Cash AR = the operator's DHL account number) gets a statement of its open airwaybills and a personal secure link. The operator ticks what was paid, gives the payment reference per AWB and attaches the deposit slip — the return opens a reconciliation sandbox automatically. Airwaybills already matched in an open or approved reconciliation are no longer open and are never sent out again.
